Pomfret Fry

Description

Pomfret, also known as Angel Fish is one of the most popular fishes that is found in India and Southeast Asia.
It has delicate white flesh with subtle “non-fishy” flavor, and that’s the reason it’s preferred more when compared to other fishes.
Pomfret Fry is a delicious fish fry recipe where the fish is marinated with a spicy paste made with a set of simple spices.

Ingredients

Fish Marinade
  • Pomfret
  • ginger
  • garlic
  • salt
  • gram flour
  • coriander powder
  • turmeric powder
  • cumin powder
  • garam masala powder
  • black pepper powder
  • red chilli powder
  • lemon juice

Procedure

Marination
  1. Add ginger, garlic in a mortal pestle and make a coarse paste.
  2. Make cuts in cleaned fish.
  3. Next, mix all the other ingredients in this paste.
  4. Apply this paste over pomfret and keep aside for 10 minutes.
Frying
  1. Heat oil for frying in a pan.
  2. One the oil, place the fish in the pan and fry from the sides for 5 minutes each until nicely browned.
  3. Remove from pan and drizzle with lemon juice and chaat masala. Serve hot.
